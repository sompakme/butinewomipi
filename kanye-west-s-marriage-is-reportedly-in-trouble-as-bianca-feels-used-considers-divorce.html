Kanye West is seemingly experiencing the career comeback he wanted. The rapper claims to have made $19 million in Yeezy sales following his low-budget Super Bowl commercial, saving him from near-bankruptcy. And his new Vultures album has received critical acclaim despite weary fans following his antisemitic behavior.

While Kanye’s wife Bianca Censori has been helping him promote the album, including attending his various listening events, there may be trouble in paradise for the couple, who married in December 2021, only a month after Ye’s divorce from Kim Kardashian was finalized.
