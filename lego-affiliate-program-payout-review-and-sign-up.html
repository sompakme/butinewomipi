  • Once logged in, navigate to the “Advertisers” tab and search for “LEGO” in the search bar. Click on the LEGO affiliate program listing to access the program details.

    The Benefits of a 7-Day Cookie Window Duration

    The LEGO Affiliate Program offers a 7-day cookie window duration, which can greatly benefit affiliates in terms of earning potential. Let’s explore the advantages of this cookie window duration.

    Firstly, the 7-day cookie window allows affiliates to earn commissions on purchases made by customers even after they initially click on the affiliate link. This means that if a customer clicks on your LEGO affiliate link and doesn’t make a purchase right away but comes back within 7 days to buy a LEGO product, you will still earn a commission. This extended time frame increases the chances of earning commissions, as customers often take time to consider their purchase decisions.

    Secondly, the 7-day cookie window duration helps affiliates overcome the challenge of cookies expiring too quickly. In some affiliate programs, cookies only last for 24 hours or even less, which can result in lost commissions if a customer doesn’t make a purchase immediately. With a 7-day cookie window, affiliates have a longer opportunity to earn commissions from their referred customers, ensuring a fairer compensation for their promotional efforts.

    Lastly, the 7-day cookie window duration aligns with the typical buying behavior of LEGO customers. LEGO products often require careful consideration and planning, especially when it comes to larger sets or special editions. By giving customers a longer period to make a purchase, the LEGO Affiliate Program maximizes the chances of conversion, allowing affiliates to earn commissions on these thoughtful and high-value transactions.
