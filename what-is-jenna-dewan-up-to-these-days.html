What Is Jenna Dewan Up To These Days?

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1046 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Jenna Dewan (formerly Jenna Dewan Tatum) had a pretty public life for a long time. She danced her way into viewers' hearts with her talent and acting prowess and left fans in an unbreakable power couple frenzy when she married fellow dancer and Step Up co-star Channing Tatum.

To the utter dismay of fans, after almost a decade together, a daughter and an almost perfect match coupling, Jenna and Channing split in 2018. This seemed like the end of love in Hollywood, but not for the actress who moved on with her new man Steve Kazee (whom she's engaged to), and a new family. So what has Jenna Dewan been up to now and how is she staying in the public scene? Let's take a look to find out all about her brand-new life.

9 Jenna Dewan's Divorce From Channing Tatum

Jenna and Channing began dating in 2006, after starring as love interests and dance partners in their film Step Up that same year. With the chemistry had onscreen, this news came as no surprise. Then came the icing on the cake when they got married in July 2009 and welcomed their baby girl Everly Tatum on June 2, 2013. Happily ever after however took a pause when the couple announced their separation in April 2018, with Jenna filing for divorce six months after. Just like Jenna, Channing Tatum has also moved on, now dating Batman actress Zoe Kravitz.

8 How Jenna Dewan Met New Fiancé Steve Kazee

What came as a surprise was when five months after the separation announcement, it was confirmed in October 2018 that Jenna was seeing fellow actor and singer Steve Kazee. The couple got engaged in February 2020. Interestingly, the Step Up actress met now fiancé years ago at a musical show "Once", where she called the brief encounter a flash with the two hitting it off almost immediately.

7 Jenna Dewan Welcomed Son And Is Spending Quality Time With Her Family

Less than 18 months of dating her fiancé, Jenna welcomed a son in March 2020. The actress made the announcement on her social media and has been frequently posting the exciting journey of her baby's growth. She has also swooned about her fiancé, with her page full of praises and sweet words to him. From her social media, the actress is enjoying her new life, and being a mother all over again, also now posting pictures of her 9-year-old daughter she rarely posted pictures of before.

6 Jenna Dewan Is Covering Magazine Covers

Apart from throwing it down on the dance floor, and taking on diverse acting roles, the actress also serves up looks on magazine covers. Jenna was glammed up as cover star for My Domaine back in June, where she showed off her beautiful and family-friendly home. The actress was also on the cover of Vulkan Magazine in April in "The Intervention Issue", as well as the spring 2022 issue of NewBeauty Magazine in March.

5 Jenna Dewan Has A Shoe Collection With DSW

The actress announced her shoe collection with DSW in April in a post she captured saying, "I am so excited to announce my new collection - Jenna Dewan × DSW!! Shop it all from heels to sandals to sneakers online at @dsw! A shoe for every occasion." She rocks and promotes them frequently on her Instagram. The 21-collection features affordable, comfortable and glamorous varieties.

4 Jenna Dewan Reprised Her Role As Lucy Lane In Superman & Lois

Jenna played Lucy Lane, younger sister of Lois Lane in CW's Supergirl. She then got to reprise her role on CW's Superman & Lois season 2 where she would be a recurring character, and the actress couldn't be more excited about it. She shared a post in June about meeting and working with her new sister Elizabeth Tulloch (who plays Lois Lane). This is the first time they will be portraying sisters onscreen.

3 Jenna Is Promoting Animal Adoption And Was Recognized For It

Jenna has been an animal rights activists for a long time, even baring it all in an ad for PETA one time. The actress' activism is still going strong and is pushing for animal adoption more than ever. She was recently honored in June by Wagmor Pets Dog Rescue at the first ever Pet Gala for her input in promoting the adoption of animals and giving them new homes.

2 Jenna Dewan Was A Judge On Come Dance With Me

The actress is living it up, all booked and busy. She announced on her social media that she was going to be part of Come Dance With Me as a judge on CBS, alongside Tricia Miranda and Dexter Mayfield back in February. The show premiered in April and came to an end in June. The actress took to her page to say how proud she was to be on the show. " ..This has been one of the joys of my life and career being part of this! I am so proud of every single team, the hard work, love and passion they have all shared with us every single week. I love you all!!!"

1 Jenna Dewan Is On ABC's The Rookie Alongside Fiancé

The actress is having a great time playing Bailey Nune and working alongside her real life fiancé Steve Kazee in ABC's The Rookie, who plays her abusive and psychotic husband. When discussing working with him, she said, "That's Steve! That's my fiancé in real life, and they asked him to come on and to play my real life husband who is a narcissistic, abusive, psychopath, basically. And it was interesting, it was fun, we had never worked together before and obviously super intense. It was such a cool experience because you get to do that, come home, and you're like, 'Oh well, I got to yell at you all day. Got that out of my system'. And now here we are. But it was wonderful."
