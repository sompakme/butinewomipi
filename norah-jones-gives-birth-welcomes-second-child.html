Norah Jones Gives Birth, Welcomes Second Child

 Posted on July 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  252 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Baby No. 2! Norah Jones has welcomed her second child, her rep confirms to Us Weekly. The uber private singer is also mom to a little boy, whom she discreetly gave birth to in February 2014 with her musician partner, whom she has not identified.

The Grammy-winning singer and her anonymous beau were spotted walking through a Los Angeles airport in late 2014 with their son. They’ve yet to reveal their first child’s name.

Although she keeps her private life under wraps, the “Come Away With Me” singer, 37, brought her son with her to a May 2014 White House event honoring military moms with Michelle Obama.

Jones, who is the daughter of late musician Ravi Shankar, has also posted Instagram photos of her little guy, but has never shown his face.

In December 2015 she shared an Instagram video of her son adorably running around to her 2013 collaborative album with Green Day’s Billie Joe Armstrong, Foreverly. “Turns out its good toddler music!” she captioned the post, along with the hashtag “he wouldn’t stop.”
