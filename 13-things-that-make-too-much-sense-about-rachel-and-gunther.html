13 Things That Make TOO Much Sense About Rachel And Gunther

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1034 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

There are certain truths that TV fans accept. Stars Hollow would be an incredible place to live (especially thanks to the food at Luke's Diner). Joey and Dawson are soulmates even if some fans want her to be with Pacey. And Ross and Rachel are an iconic couple on the sitcom Friends.

Even though it's impossible to think of this TV show not featuring this classic relationship, there is another character who has a crush on Rachel, and it's none other than Gunther. He manages the coffee shop, Central Perk, that is one of the series' main locations. Sure, Ross and Rachel are always going to be one of the major pairings here, but what if Rachel had dated someone else... and what if it had actually been Gunther?

Keep reading to find out why a Rachel/Gunther love story would be pretty logical.

13 Rachel And Gunther Were Co-Workers For A Long Time, And Many Romances Begin That Way

One reason why Rachel and Gunther make too much sense as a couple, is that they have been co-workers for a long time. It's definitely true that many relationships begin that way, as people get a chance to get to know each other and spend a lot of time together. It seems totally logical that they would have started to date.

12 They Were Actually Going To Be Roomies Once, But The Story Was Scrapped

According to Metro.co.uk, Rachel and Gunther were going to be roommates on Friends, but the story was scrapped.

This seems like it would have been cool to see... and more than that, it could have been the catalyst for romance. They would see each other all the time and bond.

11 He Has Always Loved Her And Seems More Devoted Than Ross

Ross and Rachel have always had a complicated relationship with a lot of confusion. For that reason, it seems like Gunther has been more devoted to Rachel than Ross, and it would have been interesting to see these two characters explore a relationship. When we think about it, it makes sense, right?

10 They Are Both Meant For Bigger Things Than Central Perk

Central Perk is a great place to hang out (and the Friends gang definitely spends hours there). But as a place to work? Not so much.

Rachel always disliked working there and wanted to work in fashion. Gunther is known for being pretty sad at the cafe all the time. Both characters are meant for bigger things, and they have that in common.

9 There's Less Baggage When You Date Someone Outside Your Friend Group

Since Ross and Rachel are in the same social circle, it can be a bit tough for them when they go through ups and downs.

There's less baggage when you date someone outside of your friend group, so it might be a lot more peaceful for Rachel to date Gunther instead of Ross.

8 A Fan Said Rachel's Type Isn't Gunther, But Opposites Attract!

A Friends fan posted on Quora and wrote "Gunther isn't her type." They continued that she likes people who are "handsome" and seems more focused on physical appearance.

But since opposites attract, this seems like a reason why Gunther and Rachel would make a lot of sense as a couple.

7 Gunther Is Funnier Than Ross And Would Provide More Comic Relief Than He Does

Ross complains a lot and seems sad most of the time, and he's not everyone's favorite Friends character.

Gunther seems funnier than Ross, at least in the scenes that we've been able to see of him. He would provide more comic relief. That would be great for Rachel, who is very funny herself.

6 Fans Know Rachel Dates Guys Who Have Loved Her Since Forever

Ross and Gunther actually have something in common: they both have loved Rachel for a super long time. Gunther has been into her since she started working at Central Perk and Ross, of course, has had a crush since they were younger.

Rachel seems to like this, so why couldn't she date Gunther?

5 They're Both Characters Who Are Kind Of Misunderstood

Many people think that Rachel is kind of ditzy and many think that Gunther is miserable. Since both characters can be kind of misunderstood, they have this in common, and they could bonded over this.

It would definitely be interesting to see them grow closer and get along better, right?

4 A Love Triangle Could Have Made Great TV

We love our favorite TV couples, but maybe we adore love triangles even more.

There's something so special about a love triangle, and it would have made a lot of sense for there to be a Ross/Rachel/Gunther triangle. It's easy to see how well this would have worked on the show.

3 Since Gunther Liked Rachel So Much, It Felt Like The Writers Were Gearing Up For Them To Have A Romantic Storyline

It's kind of a boring storyline for Gunther to have a one-sided crush on Rachel. The writers don't really give him anything else to work with.

Since he likes her so much, it felt like the writers were gearing up for them to fall in love. So why not? It would make a lot of sense since it felt like it was already in the works.

2 The Other Characters Would React In A Hilarious, But Supportive Way

What would happen if Rachel and Gunther started dating? Sure, the gang would think that it was pretty crazy at first... but it seems like after a while, they would be supportive.

They know Gunther and they would want Rachel to be happy, since they're really good friends to her.

1 Not Every Friends Fan Loves Ross And Rachel Together

While Rachel and Ross are a popular couple, not every fan loves them together. Many think they broke up a lot or they're not actually right for each other.

It definitely seems like a Rachel/Gunther love story could have happened on Friends, and there are many logical reasons why this would have been amazing.
