Season 8 of Summer House has arguably been one of the best because of the new cast additions and the shocking demise of Lindsay Hubbard and Carl Radke. In a recent episode of Summer House (Season 8, episode 14), Hubbard slams Radke's work ethic and career choices. Shortly after the episode's release, Hubbard double-downed on her happiness post-Radke and expressed how happy she was with her new mystery boyfriend.

With the series finale approaching, fans know the breakup between Hubbard and Radke is inevitable. The cracks in their relationship are obvious to viewers. Continue reading as we find out how Lindsay Hubbard insulted Carl Radke, what she has to say about her new boyfriend, and the ins and outs of her and Radke's demise.

Lindsay Hubbard Insulted Carl Radke (And Praised Her New Boyfriend)

In the episode "From Dusk 'Till Donne," Lindsay Hubbard and Carl Radke were at odds after Radke accepted another job offer for fellow castmate's business, Loverboy. Radke used to work for Loverboy but quit after he felt undervalued and underappreciated — putting a major rift in his friendship with Kyle Cooke.

Because of the way Radke left Loverboy, Hubbard wasn't thrilled with Radke re-joining the brand. According to Bravo TV, she wants Radke to find his passion and become successful on his own instead of choosing the easiest option.

"This is what Carl does. [He chooses] whatever's in front of his face, and it makes me question his relationship with me, honestly, because I think I was just easy and in front of his face."

She put the nail in the coffin when she said, "He does not work hard."

Radke's work ethic has been a point of contention between the two as Hubbard stated she finds hard-working men attractive and does not think Radke is "crushing life," according to Bravo.

“He’s successful in his own field and has a great job. He works very hard, is extremely driven and ambitious and, yeah, and keeps me happy in the bedroom.”

Hubbard has stayed mum on who her new boyfriend is but said on The Bitch Bible that he's a former flame who is not in the reality TV world. The two met and dated for a short time in 2020 but realized they were on two different paths. Now they've reconnected and are slowly dating again.

Lindsay Hubbard And Carl Radke Had A Very Public And Shocking Breakup

In 2016, People shared that Lindsay Hubbard and Carl Radke met on Season 1 of Summer House. The two were close friends but didn't test their romance until Season 4. However, that relationship was short-lived and they went back to being friends. Fast forward to 2021, feelings between the two friends were stirred up once again.

After her stint on Bravo's Winter House where she dropped major news about a heartbreaking miscarriage, she saw the light through the tunnel with Radke and started dating for the second time. Hubbard told People how happy she was in their relationship.

"I don't think I've ever been in a healthier relationship. Falling in love with your best friend, I don't think there's any better feeling in the world."

The two got back together after connecting at Kyle Cooke and Amanda Batuala's wedding. A year later, Radke and Hubbard made things official with an on-screen proposal. Fans saw how happy the two were before going back to the house and telling their Summer House castmates. Wedding planning quickly began, but so did the cracks in their relationship.

Bravo's newest power couple has made the dynamics within the Summer House cast extra tricky.

Until Season 8 of Summer House was released, fans of the series had no idea there were issues in the relationship. With a wedding set in stone for November 2023, the two called it quits two months prior. Carl Radke released a statement to People explaining their separation.

"We are so incredibly sorry to all of our guests for the confusion and lack of communication. We were left trying to figure out how this all exactly happened before we could even have the opportunity to determine the path forward amongst ourselves."

Shortly after Radke's statement, Hubbard shared her own on Instagram. "My entire life and future was ripped out from underneath me and I’ve had a hard time making sense of it all - with no answers or closure on why." She later said she was "humiliated" by the way the breakup happened.

Audiences soon found out that their breakup was filmed, that they would see all the highs and lows of their relationship unfold on TV, and why the wedding didn't happen. Despite their hardships, Hubbard expressed that Radke "blew up" her life and she was "blindsided."

Although they canceled the wedding, Hubbard and her friends continued with her bachelorette party to celebrate friendship, as noted by People. Likewise, all the small details were already set in stone for their Mexican destination wedding. Page Six shared that guests already had flights and hotel rooms booked. Instead of letting it all go to waste, many Bravolebrities kept their travel plans and vacationed in Mexico. Summer House cast members Kyle Cooke and Gabby Prescod documented the Mexican weekend with Radke's parents. Likewise, Vanderpump Rules stars Sheana Shay and Brock Davies were seen partying with Cooke and Prescod.

Now that the breakup between Carl Radke and Lindsay Hubbard is final, and Hubbard is newly dating, fans of Summer House are wondering what Radke will do now that he's single and back to working at Loverboy.
