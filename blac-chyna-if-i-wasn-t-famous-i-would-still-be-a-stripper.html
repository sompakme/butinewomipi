Blac Chyna: If I Wasnt Famous, I Would Still Be a Stripper

 Posted on September 12, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  468 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Could she make it rain again? Blac Chyna revealed in a Wednesday, August 17, Facebook Live interview that she believes she would still be working as a stripper if she never became famous.

Chyna, 28, who was once a dancer at Miami’s famous King of Diamonds gentlemen’s club, sat down with fiancé Rob Kardashian to chat with fans on Facebook about their upcoming E! reality series, Rob & Chyna. During their discussion, the model — who is pregnant and expecting her second child, and her first with Kardashian — spoke candidly about the X-rated beginnings of her career.

Asked what she would be doing right now if she didn’t have a huge social media following and a TV show, Chyna replied: “If I wasn’t famous. Oh God, Rob. I don’t know. I would probably be working in the strip club. I’m just kidding.”

After pausing for a moment to think about her answer, the Lashed Bar entrepreneur admitted that her talents don’t extend far beyond strutting her stuff on stage. “I would probably be still in the strip club. I’m just keeping it real,” she added. “I can’t do nothing else.”

Not that she has to worry about hopping back on the pole. The Washington, D.C., native’s rise to fame kicked off in 2010 after Drake name-dropped her in his hit single “Miss Me.” The shout-out ignited major buzz among the hip-hop community, and in November 2010 Chyna (real name: Angela Renee White) was asked to play Nicki Minaj’s stunt double in Kanye West’s “Monster” music video.

In 2011, she was cast as the leading lady in Tyga’s “Rack City” video. The pair struck up a romance and welcomed a son, King Cairo, in October 2012. Though the duo were once engaged, the rapper dumped Chyna in 2014 to date Kylie Jenner, Kardashian’s younger half-sister. Before her man left her for Jenner, 19, the makeup artist was good friends with Kim Kardashian.

The tables turned in January 2016, when the Arthur George sock designer, 29, and the video vixen went public with their relationship, a revelation that sparked a media firestorm. And in May, the unlikely couple announced that they would be welcoming a baby later this year. Naturally, their road to parenthood will be covered on Chyna and Kardashian’s forthcoming docuseries.

However, if all the notoriety were to disappear one day, the sole male Kardashian said that he would gladly follow his love back to the strip club. “That’s all right. And I would probably be in the strip club also. I would definitely go and find Chyna and go to the strip club. Eat some food,” he shared. “They got the best food in the strip club.”

Rob & Chyna premieres on E! September 11 at 9 p.m. ET.
