Who is Blueface’s Girlfriend? Recently, Los Angeles rapper, Blueface was arrested by the Las Vegas Metro Police Department on Tuesday. 

The Las Vegas Metro Police said that they had a warrant against the rapper Blueface. His arrest warrant was immediately issued because Blueface was using deadly weapons and also discharged a firearm into an occupied structure. 

The LVMPD said, “He will be booked into the Clark County Detention Center on warrants for attempted murder with a deadly weapon and discharging a firearm at/into an occupied structure.”

Blueface’s arrest was headlining on social media as the famous rapper’s arrest video was shared by TMZ. 

In the video, men dressed as civilians came toward Blueface and his girlfriend when they both were sitting outside a business located in the 300 block of Hughes Center Drive.

Blueface’s girlfriend watched as her boyfriend, Blueface, was forced against the wall by the men. Later, those men were found to be police officers and had a warrant against Blueface. 

They handcuffed him and took him under their custody around 2:40 PM. After TMZ shared his arrest video, Blueface’s girlfriend, Chrisean Rock, shared his arrest news on her Instagram story. 

In her Instagram story, she also wrote that she would be standing by her boyfriend’s side on Wednesday when he appears in court. She claimed that her boyfriend would be back home after appearing in court.

Who Is Blueface Girlfriend?

As per sources, Blueface’s girlfriend is Chrisean Rock. Both have been in a relationship for a long time and have been sighted together on multiple occasions.
