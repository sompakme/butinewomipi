Katy Perry And Ed Sheeran's Friendship Was Cemented In This Awkward Way

Highlights

  • Ed Sheeran and Katy Perry's friendship may not make sense to fans, but they shared an intimate moment in 2017.
  • Despite rumors of being best friends, the lack of recent public appearances and social media interaction suggests they may not be as close as previously implied.
  • While they may not be close friends, both Ed and Katy have a mutual bestie in Taylor Swift, who could potentially bring them closer together.

Pop royalty often tends to mix in similar circles, whether that's meeting at awards shows or being signed to the same record company, so fans will often find two or more of their favorite artists are actually friends. Some of these friendships make sense, whereas others seem downright crazy.

One of these friendships that might not make as much sense is the one between Ed Sheeran and Katy Perry. Whilst they both share an interest in music, they might not necessarily strike fans as two people who would be close friends, but this could have all changed after the pair shared a somewhat intimate moment together back in 2017.

Are Ed Sheeran And Katy Perry Friends?

Whilst there have been previous reports that the pair are best friends, there is also some evidence that this might not be the case.

For example, ever since these reports first circulated with the pair seen hanging out together at the BRITs and Ed had gatecrashed a radio interview that Katy was doing, the pair haven't been seen together since.

There is also the issue of Instagram. Whilst a following on social media doesn't necessarily indicate that two people are friends and, in the same way, two people not following each other online doesn't necessarily mean that they're not friends, neither Katy nor Ed are following each other on Instagram.

Ed only follows his HQ account, but Katy follows many of her friends, including Taylor Swift, so this may indicate that the pair aren't as close as was previously implied.

Katy And Ed Both Share A Bestie In Taylor Swift

Even if Katy and Ed aren't as close as what may have once been implied, one person that they do have in common is none other than Taylor Swift. The pair have both long been friends with Taylor separately, with Ed and Taylor releasing a song together and Katy even appeared in one of Taylor's music videos.

Despite the fact that Taylor and Katy once had a very public feud, the pair have since made up, and it could possibly be Taylor who could get both Ed and Katy to be as good friends as she is with the pair of them.

Katy Perry Asked Ed Sheeran To Smell Her Armpits

Many people will have received at least one weird request from someone else at some point in their lives, but more often than not it is likely to come from someone they are quite close to as opposed to someone they hardly know.

This didn't seem to be the case for Katy and Ed. Whilst it's not exactly clear whether this was the very first time they had met, Ed received a very weird request from Katy that might have been enough to scare anyone off, which was to smell her armpits.

If they hadn't met before then, Katy asking Ed to smell her armpits certainly would have created a bond between the pair that would be impossible to replicate with anyone else.

They started off by bonding over the Grammys and how, despite them both having multiple nominations, they both always seem to lose out to other people.

While some might think that this alone would be enough for the pair to bond over, Katy decided to take it one step further. When Ed complained about losing out every year, Katy said, "It's all right. I've lost thirteen. Smell my armpit."

As far as consolation prizes go, this is certainly one of the more unusual to be offered. Where Katy and Ed aren't known for being that close to this day, it's unknown whether it was Katy asking Ed to smell her armpit that put him off, or whether their personalities didn't quite match up.

It's more than likely that these days they are just friendly music rivals who might see each other at some awards shows or from mixing with mutual friends (such as Taylor Swift) and who are aiming to get that top spot in the charts.

Prince Wasn't A Fan Of Ed Or Katy

Despite the pair not being friends but having the armpit story to forever bond them, another thing they also have in common is that Prince wasn't a fan of either of them.

Prince had co-written a memoir before his death with Dan Piepenbring and as a part of working on this memoir, Prince had written a note about his feelings on modern-day pop music.

In this note, Prince had made it more than clear that he didn't like modern pop music, in particular calling out the likes of Ed and Katy, saying "we need to tell them that they keep trying to ram Katy Perry and Ed Sheeran down our throats, and we don't like it no matter how many times they play it."

For example, Ed was able to break records by selling a million tickets for his 2018 tour and Katy has been able to top the Billboard charts on multiple occasions, so clearly not everyone agrees with Prince's statement.
