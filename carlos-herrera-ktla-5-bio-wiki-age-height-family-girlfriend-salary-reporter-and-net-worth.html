Carlos Herrera Biography

Carlos Herrera is an American journalist born and raised in Hollywood, Los Angeles, California. Herrera presently works as a reporter for KTLA 5 News. He joined the KTLA news team in October 2020 after working at ABC 10, KXTV in the Golden State’s capital, Sacramento for for five years, he got to cover countless major stories in Northern California. He is of Latino descent and his parents are from El Salvador. He is also a member of the National Association of Hispanic Journalists.

Herrera is a very popular journalist and news reporter. He has been working at KTLA 5 as a reporter since October 2020.
