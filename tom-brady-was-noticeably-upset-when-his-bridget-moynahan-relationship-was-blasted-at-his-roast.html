Throughout Tom Brady's time in the spotlight, most of the focus was on his athletic career. However, that doesn't mean that many of Brady's followers haven't been fascinated by his personal life. After all, there have been reports about Gisele Bündchen coming between Tiger Woods and Brady's friendship and what it is like to date Brady.

Since his divorce from Bündchen, there have also been reports about how Brady has refused to put up with any drama. That may be why Brady seemed so upset when his Netflix roast included a joke about his past relationship with Bridget Moynahan.

This article will reveal how Tom Brady reacted after a roast joke about his relationship with Bridget Moynahan. Then, the article will look at the moment during the roast when Brady seemed upset by a Robert Kraft joke during his roast.

Nikki Glaser's Joke About Tom Brady's Relationship With Bridget Moynahan Seemed To Upset The Football Great

Over the decades, comedy roasts have become a staple of the entertainment industry. For a celebrity to be chosen to be the topic of a roast is widely considered to be a huge honor by many people.

That said, anyone who has watched footage of a roast should know that they are incredibly harsh events. Whether Tom Brady knew what he was in for during his 2024 roast, a joke Nikki Glaser madeabout his relationship with Bridget Moynahan seemed to shock him.

Before Tom Brady married Gisele Bündchen, and they had two children together, he dated Moynahan for months. When the former couple broke up, it turned out that Moynahan was carrying Brady's child.

Months after their split, Moynahan gave birth to Brady's eldest child, his son John "Jack" Moynahan. Since then, Brady has been an active part of his eldest son's life. Brady also has maintained a residence in New York City where Jack Moynahan primarily lives with his mother, Bridget Moynahan.

When she took the stage at Brady's roast, Nikki Glaser made a pretty brutal joke about the circumstances surrounding the birth of the athlete's eldest child.

"Tom, you're the best to ever play for too long, you retired then came back then retired, I get it,' the stand-up, 39, said. 'It's hard to walk away from something that isn't your pregnant girlfriend."

Up until this point in Glaser's joke, the comedian made it seem like Brady intentionally broke up with Bridget Moynahan when she was pregnant. In reality, however, Brady seemingly was completely unaware that Moynahan was carrying his child when they split.

To Glaser's credit, she played up the fact that Brady didn't know Moynahan was pregnant to make fun of the football great even more.

"To be fair, he didn't know. He just thought she was getting fat."

From there, Glaser went on to joke about Brady infamously obsessing over his diet. As Glaser joked, Brady's extreme diet resulted in him losing the family he had with Gisele Bündchen.

After that joke, the roast's producers cut to a wide shot that included Brady. As a result, viewers got to see that Brady looked upset as he spoke to the person seated beside him seemingly about Glaser's jokes.

Of course, there is no way to know for sure that Brady was upset at that moment. However, it is easy to understand why Brady may have been upset that Glaser brought up his three children in a roundabout way.

Was Tom Brady Really Mad At Jeff Ross' Roast Joke About Robert Kraft?

Between the years 2000 and 2019, Tom Brady made a staggering amount of money as the quarterback for the New England Patriots. Throughout that time of almost two decades, Patriots owner Robert Kraft was the one who helped make Brady incredibly rich and famous.

Given the roles that Brady and Kraft had in each other's lives, it would make sense for the two men to feel mutual loyalty. It seems like that loyalty survived Brady going on to play for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers and then retiring.

Given Brady and Kraft's bond, the former football star should have expected there to be jokes about Kraft during his roast. Still, for many viewers, it seemed like Brady was mad that Ross joked about Kraft.

In 2019, Kraft was charged with two counts of soliciting following an investigation into Florida massage parlors. Ultimately, Kraft was cleared of all charges but the situation was such a big controversy that football fans certainly haven't forgotten.

Before Tom Brady's roast was streamed live on Netflix in 2024, the service held a similar event. In 2021, Netflix users got the chance to watch the roast of The Jonas Brothers.

As a result of how memorable this incident was, Jeff Ross made a joke about it. While describing a theoretical conversation between Brady and Kraft, Ross pretended to be the football star asking the Patriots' owner if he wanted a massage.

Several seconds after Ross' joke got a big laugh, the comedian was milking the response. At that moment, it seemed like Brady believed Ross was going to continue talking about Kraft's massage controversy. As a CBS Sports article about the roast describes, Brady got out of his chair, walked over to Ross who was still at the microphone, and told the comedian, "Don't say that s**t again."

After the roast, Jeff Ross made an appearance on The Rich Eisen Show. Unsurprisingly, Eisen asked Ross if Brady really was mad at his joke about Kraft. In response, Ross stated that Brady wasn't actually mad and claimed the football legend was just playing along.

"Come on. Come on. No way. No way. He was having fun. He was having fun, you know? It's like, that's his dad, you know? That's like, Robert Kraft is like a father figure to him, you know? He was just showing his love for Robert Kraft."

Ross also told Eisen that Robert Kraft loved the joke. "And Robert Kraft loved it. We had a great talk afterward, and he was so happy that I gave him a shout-out and a salute."
