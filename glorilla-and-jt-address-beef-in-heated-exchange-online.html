GloRilla And JT Address Beef In Heated Exchange Online

 Posted on September 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  506 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

GloRilla and JT sort of addressed their alleged beef, stemming from a rumor that the CMG rapper slapped the City Girl over ignoring her at the 2023 MTV Video Music Awards. The two recently had an exchange on X after Glo dropped a new mixtape titled Ehhthang Ehhthang where she mentions JT on “Aite.”

The lyrics in question are: “I just pray one day the bad bi**hes would come together/ ’Cause Cardi and Nicki on a track would break some f**kin’ records/ Me and JT ain’t the best of friends but we ain’t beefin’/ Know bi**hes always havin’ [animosity] that they keep a secret.”

JT hopped on X to respond to the line while also claiming that Glo is all about “fake unity.”

“I been said she didn’t! She the one went radio silent!” JT wrote. “Played into, released a song about slapping rap bi**hes now it’s female unity?? CORNY!!!!!” She then addressed the rumor of Glo assaulting her and why she feels the “Yeah Glo” rapper never shut it down.

“I guess she was waiting on her tape to address & who gone clear up going viral for slapping someone when the whole hood believed it! It was on brand!” JT projected, to which Glo responded, “Ho* shut yo dumb a** up & fix dem [ugly a**] wigs I said it ain’t no beef ho you da one with da secret animosity.”

In another post she wrote, “Nvm no free promo.”

The exchange began heating up as Glo claimed that she did indeed hit JT “with a purse,” and also put hands on others. The latter prompted JT to post a photo of all of her mugshots, with a ninth one “pending” — implying that she might have to catch another case.

“Don’t make me pop up at one of them Backyard BBQ shows you doing,” Glo wrote in another post. “You feeling a way about SLAPPING RAP BI**HES AND MAKING BAIL HO. Guess that last run in had you thinking you da only bi**h I touched?”

She went on, “I mentioned yo name to clear the air! What you wanted me to say I didn’t slap you but I hit you with a purse instead??? Scary a** ho.”

JT called her bluff with the response, “OMG you are a liarrrr! Bi**h you didn’t hit me with sh*t FANNED out a** bi**h you approach me sounding like an old beat up box Chevy ‘WHAT UP GANG’.”

“I’m the most supportive bi**h ever with receipts!” she continued. “But some where in a bi**h air a** head I’m their competition! Out of ALL these bi**hes who d*ck you ride you wanna pick me as your enemy L O S E R!”

The two ended the exchange with Glo proclaiming herself as the winner of the beef, and JT threatening to “poke holes” in her like an air mattress.

“The last one will be from me poking holes in you like the air mattresses you were sleeping on,” JT wrote before Glo replied, “Yo comebacks weak just stop boo it’s ok I won.
