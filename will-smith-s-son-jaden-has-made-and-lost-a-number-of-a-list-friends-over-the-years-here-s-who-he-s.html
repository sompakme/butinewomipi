Will Smith's Son Jaden Has Made And Lost A Number Of A-List Friends Over The Years, Here's Who He's

 Posted on September 7, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1186 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Jaden Smith used to be known for his acting and rapping careers, but his presence on social media has certainly been his main method of fame as of late. He used to go viral for his mysterious tweets, but over the past few years, he's been much more direct with his thoughts and opinions.

Related: While Filming Life In A Year, Jaden Smith Said His Hotel Spiked His Food And Kicked Him Out

Regardless of his talents and tweets, at the end of the day, most people know Smith because of who he's connected to. There's his superstar family, consisting of one of the biggest couples in the world Will and Jada. His sister Willow had a smash hit in "Whip My Hair".

Jaden also has some famous companions as well. Here are some of his biggest friendships over the years, including who he's connected to now, and which of his former friends he has beef with now.

8 Is Jaden Smith Still Friends With August Alsina After The Jada Pickett-Smith Controversy?

August Alsina might be remembered for his romance with Jada Pickett-Smith, but his friendship with Jaden for whatever reason hasn't been discussed as much. Jaden and August have a pretty interesting history together - here's exactly what they've done together.

Per Jojo Crews, Smith and Alsina met as actors for the unaired TV show Brothers in Atlanta. After that, they would attend music festivals and Hollywood parties together. Naturally, things went downhill after all the drama that transpired. However, if it wasn't for what was once a pretty close friendship between those two, perhaps Will and Jada's relationship troubles would have gone differently.

August has been kept out of the limelight for the most part since he revealed he had a boyfriend in 2022.

7 Is Jaden Smith Friends With Tyler, The Creator?

Smith has become really close to rapper Tyler, the Creator. They've been friends for some time, but over the last few years, things have gotten so good between them that some fans believe they may have been in a relationship at some point.

According to Distractify, Jaden and Tyler were seen hanging out back in 2013. Then, in 2015, Smith tweeted out "I Think I'm In Love With You" to Tyler on his birthday. At that time, most fans believed the two were innocently messing with each other. However, Tyler confessed a few times on his albums "Flower Boy" and "Igor" that he's seriously into guys in a romantic way, once again sparking rumors of a fling between him and Smith.

Jaden then said at a concert in 2018 that Tyler is his "boyfriend", something that Tyler would deny later on social media and in an interview with Nardwuar. Regardless, they are certainly close friends with each other, even if they aren't exactly dating. Tyler is friends with other A-list celebrities such as Kendall Jenner, so it shouldn't be a surprise he's pretty close to Jaden.

6 Is Jaden Smith Friends With Kid Cudi?

Kid Cudi is famously friends with celebrities such as Kanye West, Selena Gomez, and even Timothee Chalamet. However, two of his closest celebrity friends have been the Smith siblings, both Jaden and Willow.

Related: This Awkward Exchange Had Fans Cringing For Selena Gomez And Kid Cudi

The first time Cudi and Smith were seen together was in Kid Cudi's 2016 music video "Surfin'", where Jaden, Willow, and a couple of other celebrities appeared as background dancers. Jaden and Cudi then made actual music together when Cudi featured on Jaden's song "On My Own".

Jaden recently attended Cudi's 39th birthday party alongside other stars such as Willow and Jenna Ortega, so their friendship is still alive and well.

5 Is Jaden Smith Friends With Justin Bieber?

Justin and Jaden are around the same age, and they both were child superstars who hit it big at the same time. Smith may have been famous way before Bieber, but his starring role in 2010's Karate Kid was what sealed his fate as an A-lister.

Meanwhile, Justin had his first #1 hit in 2009's "Baby" song featuring Ludacris. Since then, he's had a variety of hit songs, as well as some famous relationships with people like Selena Gomez and Hailey Bieber.

Related: Did Justin Bieber Really Date Kendall Jenner?

Of course, Bieber and Smith linked up in 2010's "Never Say Never" song that went all the way to #8 on the Billboard Hot 100. Since then, the two haven't been as close to each other, but there are a few instances of them hanging out. Bieber did wish Jaden a happy birthday in 2021 on his Instagram Story, proof that things are certainly fine between them.

4 Is Jaden Smith Friends With Jordyn Woods?

The friendship between Jaden and Jordyn isn't talked about much, but its influence on pop culture has been undeniably huge.

Back in 2019, Woods appeared on Jada Pickett-Smith's podcast Red Table Talks. This was due to her longtime friendship with the Smith family, in particular her close relationship with Jaden.

According to ET Online, Jordyn met Jaden when they were children due to her family's relationship with the Smiths. Jaden and Jordyn hit it off as children, and Woods would eventually meet Kylie through Jaden.

Woods isn't expected to ever make amends with Jenner after what they went through. However, Woods is still friends with Jaden despite all the drama that's occurred.

3 Is Jaden Smith Still Friends With Jackie Chan After 2010's Karate Kid?

Jaden famously starred in The Pursuit of Happiness with his father Will. However, being the lead character in 2010's Karate Kid was what cemented his star power. Jackie Chan also starred in that film, leaving many fans wondering if the two are still friends over a decade later.

Related: These Jackie Chan Movies Made An Absolutely Insane Amount Of Money

There isn't much known about their relationship nowadays, but rumors about a sequel or spinoff have been swirling on the internet lately. According to FandomWire, Chan has privately denied a role in whatever Karate Kid project is coming next. As unlikely as it is that Smith was to return, if he does it won't be alongside Chan.

2 Is Jaden Smith Still Friends With Kylie Jenner After They Dated?

These two have long been rumored to have dated back in the day, but was anything ever confirmed? According to The Sun, the two dated back in high school. However, the romance didn't last long. They've reverted to friendship status, which is where their relationship stands to this day.

1 Does Jaden Smith Have Beef With Kanye West And Drake, Or Are They Friends?

Drake and Kanye have numerous references to Will Smith in their songs. But what exactly is their relationship with Will's son Jaden?

Jaden publicly revealed in 2018 that he wasn't cool with Drake and Kanye because of one reason, and that was his rap career. Smith told Complex that once his album SYRE peaked at #24 on the Billboard 200 chart, Kanye, Drake, and even Donald Glover changed their attitudes toward him. Because of that, things became awkward.
