 Posted on July 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  91 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon


Matt Hardy will work for International Wrestling Cartel on 7/1 in a non-wrestling role in Monroeville, PA.

Jim Cornette has taken a leave of absence from WWE’s developmental system. There is some backstage heat on him from WWE management although all the details are not known at this time. Tommy Dreamer announced yesterday that for the next 4-6 weeks, he would be writing OVW television. We’ll keep you posted. (Partial Source: PWinsiers)

Head RAW writer Brian Gerwitz is also on a leave of absence from WWE at the current time.
