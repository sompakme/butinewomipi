Wrestling is probably what gave rise to other forms of combat, including martial arts and different forms of unarmed fighting. WWE is the world's most successful wrestling entertainment brand and the largest wrestling promotion company in the world.

The popularity of wrestling and WWE has given rise to a group of wrestling superstars, who are in every way some of the toughest wrestlers in the United States and a few other countries. These superstars are just like the movie stars and music idols because they are just as famous as they are and some earn as much as or even more than some movie stars.
