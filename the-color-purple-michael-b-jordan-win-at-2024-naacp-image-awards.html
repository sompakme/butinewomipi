'The Color Purple,' Michael B. Jordan Win At 2024 NAACP Image Awards

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  438 words  |  Martina Birk

Tonight (March 13), the NAACP kicked off night three of the 55th NAACP Image Awards announcing the third round of winners in the non-televised award categories, which include Outstanding Animated Series, Documentary, Independent Motion Picture, and Ensemble Cast in a Motion Picture, and more. The awards were announced tonight during the NAACP Virtual Image Awards’ Show sponsored by Wells Fargo, Google, Bank of America, Gushers, FedEx, Airbnb, Amazon, Diageo, Denny’s, Alaska Airlines, and American Airlines at  naacpimageawards.net.

Tonight’s winners included The Color Purple for Outstanding Ensemble Cast in a Motion Picture, High on the Hog: How African American Cuisine Transformed America for Outstanding Documentary (Television), Invisible Beauty for Outstanding Documentary (Film), Leah Sava Jeffries for Outstanding Performance by a Youth (Series, Special, Television Movie or Limited-Series) – Percy Jackson and the Olympians, Michael B. Jordan for Outstanding Guest Appearance – Saturday Night Live, Michelle Buteau for Outstanding Breakthrough Creative (Television) – Survival of the Thickest, and more.

The night additionally featured host Khleo Thomas and appearances from presenters, including Brian Jordan (Tyler Perry’s Sistas), Marcel Spears (CBS’ The Neighborhood), and Nyambi Nyambi (NBC’s Night Court and  CBS’ Mike and Molly). The three-day-long 55th NAACP Image Awards Virtual Experience concluding tonight, invites fans to join the Image Awards in a celebration of the outstanding achievements and performances of people of color across more than 90 competitive categories. 

The “55th NAACP Image Awards,” hosted by Queen Latifah, will premiere LIVE on Saturday, March 16 at 8:00 PM ET/PT on BET and the CBS Television Network.
