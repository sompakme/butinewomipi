The Truth About Dina Lohan's Internet Boyfriend, Jesse Nadler

 Posted on September 11, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  687 words  |  Larita Shotwell

After starring in the hit movie Mean Girls and making $1 million, Lindsay Lohan now has a net worth of $800,000, and she doesn't appear in many movies anymore. Her mom, Dina Lohan, has recently been in the spotlight herself, and it's all because of the man she has been dating.

Dina Lohan and Jesse Nadler have been a couple for five years, which is definitely a long time, but there are some things that people need to know about this love story.

A Catfish?

While some fans wonder if Catfish is real, the show examines online relationships, which is something that Lohan has been in for a while now.

Back in February 2019, Dina Lohan went on Celebrity Big Brother and said she had fallen in love. According to Us Weekly, the couple has been engaged twice.

In June 2020, Lohan said that they were getting married, as Nadler sent her an engagement ring. He explained to Us Weekly, “My plan is to fly home to Long Island, get down on one knee and propose. Soon after, set the wedding date in the Hamptons — my hometown. I just knew from the first call we had a bond and a calmness between us. From the beginning, we had this feeling between us that neither one of us could explain. It felt so real and right."

The problem? People aren't sure that Nadler is who he says he is. Because they haven't met each other, the word "catfish" has mentioned. Nev Schulman, the host of the long-running MTV reality series Catfish (and the documentary of the same name), said he would help find out the real Jesse Nadler.

According to People, Nadler said he doesn't wish to be known as a catfish. He said to Page Six, “I’m a good guy. I’m the real deal. Just the thought of people thinking I could be a catfish when everyone knows me and knows how hard I work and how I respect women and mothers, it just kills me.”

Lindsay Lohan tweeted her support of her mom on Celebrity Big Brother but mentioned the word catfish as well: "But no more weird #catfish please," according to People.com.

According to People, the reason why Nadler and Lohan have never actually met is that his mom isn't in good health.

Nadler has a job in commercial real estate.

Back Together?

According to Page Six, Nadler said that he isn't sure that the romance is going as well as it once was. He said, “She doesn’t want me to go, but I need her to live a healthy life,” Nadler told us. “It really comes down to that. I want a healthy, solid woman as a wife.” He continued, “I’ll set up ‘my house’ in Westhampton Beach. I wish I was setting up ‘home’ with Dina, but I don’t think it’s in the cards."

The couple broke up in April 2020 and then got back together. A source told People that Lohan thought that Nadler just wanted to be famous and that he wasn't interested in meeting IRL. The source said, “there was no meeting scheduled” and “Dina was already suspicious that he was media hungry and she is in talks about doing several new shows, including a dating show.”

Regardless of where Lohan and Nadler currently stand, they have both had some struggles in the past. In April 2020, the pair reunited as they had stopped drinking. Nadler told Page Six,  “[Dina’s] been sober and healthy for over two months, and we’ve been great. I don’t drink anymore."

Nev Schulman told ET Online that the plan was to get Dina Lohan on an episode of Catfish but the stars didn't align.

Schulman said he might get the information out there somehow: he said, "there's a lot more to it than just Dina, and I think it's an important story that needs to be told. So I think I'm going to write an article or some sort of piece about that whole situation and hopefully, you'll see that soon."
