Anna Faris Opens Up About High-Risk Pregnancy, Baby Jack

 Posted on August 4, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  787 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Time to talk. Anna Faris opened up in Health‘s April 2015 cover story about her high-risk pregnancy struggle, and why she’s finally deciding to discuss it openly.

“Jack’s doing so well,” Faris, 38, said of her 2-year-old son with husband Chris Pratt. “I think that’s why we can talk about it now a little bit. He’s just so happy, bright and funny. For a while I thought maybe I’d be a stay-at-home mom for a couple of years. But in Hollywood, it’s not as though it’s always a choice, you know? You feel very little control over the trajectory of your own career.”

The couple endured a huge scare with their son in August 2012, after Jack was born two months ahead of his due date. “I was having an uneventful, happy pregnancy, and so it was really a huge surprise that my water broke in the middle of the night,” she recalled. “I wish we didn’t have this biological clock. I’m thinking we have to start talking about it, looking at all kinds of options. I’m so lucky that I have a partner in Chris who’s in the same industry and really understands what’s required of me in terms of time commitment. But I was like, ‘Oh man, this s–t is f—ing hard.’”

Still, the Mom actress and Pratt really want to expand their family of three. “Very much so,” Faris said. “But it will definitely be a whole new complication because now I’m, like, a high-risk pregnancy, so it requires some different monitoring. My doctor’s like, ‘I don’t think you’ll have to be on bed rest the whole time.’ And I’m like, ‘I’m on a show.'”

Faris also shared with Health that she’s currently shaping up — thanks to the help of her husband. “He makes me feel really beautiful even when I’m feeling disgusting,” Faris gushed of the Guardians of the Galaxy hunk. “I’ll be in boxer shorts and a grubby T-shirt with greasy hair, and he’ll still be like, ‘Whoa, honey. Whoaaa.'”

It also helps that the Jurassic Park actor, 35, has been vigilant about maintaining his buff body. “Things are working in my favor right now,” Faris noted. “My job is so physical. I just don’t have time to sit down. I’ll get, like, a 15-minute break to eat a little bit, and then they’re like, ‘C’mon, we have to go!’ The other thing is that Chris is on a diet, which is helping me.'”

Working out with her husband, however, is not her forte. “When I work out with Chris at home, we go through a little routine,” she explained. “He has me do lunges and stuff, and the lunges get hard after a while. It’s really frustrating when someone you love is telling you how to improve your form!” And despite her personal vendetta against workouts, Faris says nothing in the world makes her happier than spending time with her family.

“Chris and I took Jack to the train museum. There were paparazzi, but we were just like, ‘It’s an odd thing that’s happening, but let’s not stress or stress Jack out,'” she explained. “At one point, Chris turned to me and said, ‘I don’t think I’ve ever been happier in my whole life.’ And I felt the same way.”

Unlike some of her Hollywood peers, Faris said she has an alternate perspective about paparazzi. “Most of them are pretty nice guys, just trying to make a living, and a lot of them are apologetic,” she said. “I think some celebrities are so mean to them and resent them so much. I mostly feel scared just going out without makeup on. But at the same time, I don’t want it to force a lifestyle change.”

Faris joked that some people she works with want her to dress better. “They’re like, ‘Can you please not wear sweatpants everywhere you go?'” she said with a laugh. “The scary part is that vulnerability of like, ‘Oh man, I’m gonna be in ‘Stars Without Makeup’ again!'”

Even with her pretty face and great physique, Faris humbly noted that her beauty was “all surface” to Health. “I make sure my roots are good — although right now they’re not,” she shared. “I would love to be in a place where somebody comes to my house and just, like, spray-tans me and does my roots. But I just don’t have the patience for it. I don’t want to spend my free time doing that stuff. Even with massage these days, I’m like, ‘Ugh.’ I like to unwind with some wine and movies, and spend time with Chris and Jack.”
