Billy Ray Makes Sweet Post About Miley Cyrus Amid Strained Relationship

Billy Ray Cyrus seems to be trying to repair his relationship with Miley Cyrus, and has taken to Instagram to do so. The father-daughter relationship has been strained ever since he and Tish Cyrus divorced, with Miley most recently pointedly snubbing her dad in her speech when she won her first Grammy.

The country star posted a throwback to a picture of him holding baby Miley in his arms in what he called one of his favorite moments in life.

"One of my best memories ever: @cma [Country Music Association] Fest, back when it was Fan Fair. That's @mileycyrus and me, surrounded by thousands of incredible fans at the fairgrounds. The next day, someone handed me this picture, and I wrote that poem right then on the bus," he wrote. There were pictures of the handwritten poem, which the musician dubbed "The Moment."

Billy Ray then went on to praise his daughter. "I’m incredibly proud of her," he stated. "She's a survivor and a true artist. She learned early on to love and appreciate the fans who make everything possible. We both cherish the connection we have with our fans and are grateful for every single one of you! Hope you all have a great Friday! Now rock the country!!!"

Fans Are Rooting For A Reconciliation

Fans were moved by Billy Ray's post, and seeing him gush over his daughter apparently made them wish for a reconciliation. While it seems a lot of people blame the country singer for the estrangement, they believe he should get a chance to repair his relationship with Miley Cyrus.

"I hope you two work it out even after all that you've done," one user commented on his post. Someone else wrote "I hope she forgives you and you can build a healthy relationship after all."
