Jiggly Caliente is a Filipino-American drag performer mainly known for competing on the fourth season of RuPaul’s Drag Race and her recurring role as Veronica Ferocity on the series Pose.

Her debut album T.H.O.T Process was released on March 9, 2018.

She has officially joined as a judge in Drag Race Philippines.

Jiggly Caliente Teeth Before And After

During her appearance in the drag race, Caliente was often teased for her less-than-perfect teeth.

In 2016, Caliente had a cosmetic dental procedure; after that, she has become much more confident and is smiling, flashing her perfect set of teeth.

With her perfect teeth, Caliente had a glow-up when she returned to the sixth season of RuPaul’s Drag Race All Stars; not only did her look, but her performance was also better, and she was more confident while performing. In the show’s fourth season, she was unsure of herself, but in the sixth season, she is a whole new person.

Jiggly Caliente Nationality And Real Name

Her real name is Bianca Castro; she was born on November 29, 1980, in Laguna, Philippines. She had moved to America in 1991 with her mother and brother. She is an American nationality.

She has not given much information about her personal life. She had posted a picture of her mother on her Instagram on Mother’s Day.

Judge Of RuPaul’s Drag Race Jiggly Caliente’s Net Worth

According to Famous Birthdays, Caliente’s estimated net worth is $1.5 million. She is one of a well-known reality TV stars.

She started her career in Drag Race when she participated in the fourth season of RuPaul’s Drag Race on November 13, 2011.

Apart from Drag Race, in 2015, she started her acting career when she played a role as a shop owner in the season two finale of Broad City. she appeared in the pilot episode of Search Party.

In the season finale episode of Playing House, she played her part with fellow Drag Race alumni Bob The Drag Queen, Katya, and Detox.

She has also appeared in the series Pose as Veronica for the sixth and eighth episodes of the first season. In 2018, she appeared on Saturday Night Live with Drag Race alumn Peppermint as a Drag Queen for Garmin GP-Yasss sketch. She co-hosted a Translation talk show hosted by an all-trans cast in 2020.

She released her first single on March 1, 2018, on her official Vevo account. Her debut album T.H.O.T Process features Drag Race alumni  Sharon Needles, Peppermint, Alaska, Ginger Minj, Phi Phi O’Hara, and Manila Luzon.
