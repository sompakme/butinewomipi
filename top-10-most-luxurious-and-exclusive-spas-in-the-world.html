Spa-goers have really become better traveled and more sophisticated. More is expected from every spa experience. What is interesting is that spa-goers are now more than willing to spend any amount just to experience complete luxury and exclusivity in a spa. They could easily travel around the world to find the perfect spa experience.

No wonder, luxury and exclusive spas are now racing against each other to entice customers. Spas are now not only about massages. Competitive spas from around the world are adding private suites, rain showers, unique baths, exotic treatments, fitness programs, and traditional therapies from far-flung cultures in their offerings.

Changes at modern spas are somehow dramatic. Luxury spas have started reaching out to a new niche in potential guests. For instance, men are highly targeted these days through modification of spa menus, changing oils and making the treatments less girly. Spa resorts are also targeting families to entice more luxury vacationers from around the world.

Spa resorts and services are evolving. Traditional mud baths are passé. Spa-goers have become more discerning because there is an intense competition among spa resorts. Here are the top 10 of the world’s most luxurious and exclusive spas.

10. Six Senses Hideaway

Located amid the spectacular Zighy Bay in Musandam Peninsula in Oman is the Six Senses Hideaway. It has nine treatment rooms where full selections of physical treatments are offered, mostly concentrated on rejuvenation and holistic wellness. Right from the time you arrive through any of the dramatic methods available (by stunning mountain drive, speedboat, or paragliding), you would instantly realize that this spa resort is truly anything but conventional.

9. Peter Island’s Spa

The British Virgin Islands could be an ideal spa destination, thanks to Peter Island’s Spa. The resort sits luxuriously in a private island, where stunning sceneries of pristine seas and lush hillsides are combined to offer one of the best sights in the entire Caribbean. Wait until you get your spa treatment in any of the 12 rooms that are each equipped with its own steam room, swimming pool, and private Jacuzzi. This spa also offers a combination of ancient traditions and modern techniques in spa treatments. It aims to arouse the senses while soothing your soul.

8. Round Hill

Sophisticated, sublime, and subdued; this is what the Jamaican resort experience is all about. The luxury hotel located in Montego Bay radiates as a gem of perfection in this interesting corner of the world. Round Hill offers impeccable service coupled with the generally warm Jamaican hospitality. The oceanfront guest rooms within the private villa suite are designed by Ralph Lauren. The main attractions are of course the relaxing and luxurious spa treatments.

7. Villa Marjina

Egypt is well represented in this department. Located within the Mount Sinai peninsula is the luxurious Villa Marjina. It is an elegantly furnished villa exuding the contemporary Egyptian style. For vacationers, there is a superb range of sophisticated amenities. The discreet hotel boasts of its spa facilities and treatments that truly facilitate relaxation and unwinding.

6. Royal Westmoreland

This exceptional Caribbean hideaway is a five-star golf, spa, and beach resort. It is settled in the lush landscaped grounds of St. James parish in Barbados. Guests are given exclusive privileges to use all facilities, including a Sanctuary Spa complex with swimming pool as well as sundeck. As a residential development, it offers the best things in life to homeowners and guests alike. Royal Westmoreland seems to have perfected the art of blending luxury, privacy, and excellent vacation services.

5. Ezzahra

This elegant holiday accommodation in Morocco is actually a huge private villa that offers luxury like no other. Originally constructed as a family retreat, the building is set amid an extensive garden with a large swimming pool, shaded terraces, a gym, a paddle tennis court, and screening/game rooms. Of course, its spa facilities are the main enticements, coming with in-house therapists. The privacy, exclusivity, and stylishness are what make this spa facility truly remarkable.

4. Kaparelli House

The resort is more of a luxury house for guests. It is thoughtfully equipped to bring about the privileges of a typical luxury home. The experience it brings could easily be relaxing and memorable. The amenities are outstanding: an outdoor Jacuzzi, a gym, children’s play room, and a spa room. Spa treatments are offered and facilitated in the spa room, which comes fully equipped with steam shower, sauna, and massage table.

3. Elounda Gulf Villas

Set in the serene Crete Island in Greece, this exclusive luxury resort is close to the breathtaking Elounda beach. The seclusion is a main enticement aside from its being a 5-star hotel, a Small Luxury Hotels of the World member. Its exclusive Royal Spa Villa is exquisitely ornamented to facilitate holiday extravagance. It comes with a gym, a steam bath, a sauna, and a huge seasonally heated swimming pool.

2. Al Maha Desert Resort & Spa

Within the Dubai Desert Conservation Reserve in the United Arab Emirates is this luxury resort. It is nestled in the verdant palm oasis. To begin with, Al Maha is a beautiful showcase of the architecture and heritage of the Bedouins. Its Timeless Spa is strategically set amid a tranquil environment. Holistic treatments are offered from the wisdom and traditions of Middle Eastern and South East Asian cultures. Don’t miss the aromatherapy as well as Rasoul treatments, which are among this resort’s signature spa offerings.

1. Blue & Green: The Lake Spa Resort

The elegant 5-star spa resort is one of the main attractions in Villamoura, the biggest luxury European tourism complex located in Algarve, Portugal. The place is designed specifically for leisure. Its setting provokes and lures discovery. Blue & Green Spa boasts of its theme: ‘Five Continents and five senses.’ A wide range of spa treatments are offered not just to stimulate one’s senses but also to nourish the soul. Also included in its list of spa facilities are an indoor treatment pool, a fitness center, and a hair studio.
