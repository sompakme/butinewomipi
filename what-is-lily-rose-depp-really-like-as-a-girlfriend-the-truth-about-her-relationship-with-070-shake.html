Lily-Rose Depp's love life was always going to attract attention given her famous parents, but the partners she chooses make it all the more interesting. Over the years, she has been linked to several famous men, including Austin Butler and Timothee Chalamet. While she has been photographed looking cozy alongside several men, there is something different about her most recent relationship.

Depp is currently dating rapper 070 Shake, and she's not afraid to go public with it either. But what is it really like to date her? The differences between her couplings with 070 Shake And Timothee Chalamet are glaring …

What Was Lily-Rose Depp's Relationship With Timothee Chalamet Really Like?

Lily-Rose Depp is the daughter of Johnny Depp and Vanessa Paradis. Was she always destined for a career in show business? Perhaps, so when she landed a role in The King, no one was shocked. She and Timothee Chalamet are believed to have bonded when cast on the film n 2018.

During their reported relationship, they were photographed together on several occasions, including sharing a kiss in New York City.

An Us Weekly insider commented on the kiss, telling the publication, "They were pretty playful and flirty with each other" and looked like "a new young couple in love."

Paparazzi caught another of their special moments, and this was when they were on a yacht in Capri, Italy. Despite their obvious hangouts, neither star made an official statement about their time together, and their dating timeline remains unclear. That said, they did appear to have fun.

Chalamet also seemed to have high praise for Depp as he hinted at their relationship in an interview with GQ magazine. "I was on this boat all day with someone I really loved, and closing my eyes, I was like, indisputably, 'That was great,'" he said of the yacht trip. So, what do we know about their time together? Well, the couple were in love, and both appear to be incredibly passionate about the people they are with. Yet despite this, most of their relationship remains a mystery.

Their relationship would end in 2020 after spending more than a year together (in celebrity terms, this could be considered a serious relationship).

As for what it's like to date Chalamet? Well, for starters, he doesn't like that word. "Date is very much a scary word, because then that context has been established," he said in a 2018 interview with W Magazine. "You can always see people on early date behavior."

Who Is Lily-Rose Depp Dating Now?

Lily-Rose Depp has been linked to 070 Shake (whose real name is Danielle Balbuena). Their relationship is still new, as they were only connected in 2023. However, they appear to be incredibly happy together. There is also something different about their time together: Depp has shared photos of her girlfriend on social media (a decision she had never made before).

Depp has been someone to shy away from confirming relationships, and she has been wary about sharing her life with fans (and rightfully so). But she confirmed their relationship in March in a move no one saw coming. She did this by posting a photo of them together on her Instagram story and referred to the rapper as her "crush."

The photo also revealed they had been together for four months (and appear to be still going strong at the time of publication).

You won't get much from 070 Shake's social media because they don't post often, and what they have shared has been related to their music. However, Depp has been more open. She has also shared an Instagram story referring to the rapper as the "love of my life" (via People). And she's posted a photo of them kissing.

All this is to say that Depp has gone from not posting anything from her previous relationships to feeling comfortable sharing this coupling with fans.

What Has Lily-Rose Depp Said About Dating?

Fans will notice the difference in how Lily-Rose Depp is sharing her relationship. It may come as a surprise to some because she has always valued her privacy. She spoke about this on an appearance on The Drew Barrymore Show in 2021.

"I think that the value of privacy is something that's been instilled in me from an early age," she said. "Growing up in a family like I did ... I always learned the importance of privacy and valuing that and keeping things just for yourself. And so that's something that I've carried on into my own career, and that's really important to me."

Lily-Rose Depp has spoken about how she identifies in an interview with Nylon in 2016. "It's not that big of a deal," she said about posing for a photo shoot with individuals who don't "consider themselves 100% straight" (via People).
