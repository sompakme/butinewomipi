Owen Wilson's Net Worth Is High But He Has Had A Troubled Life

Owen Wilson has a $70 million net worth, according to Celebrity Net Worth, and he has made money from his impressive resume of comedic movies. But even though he has such a high net worth, of course that doesn't mean that he hasn't struggled.

While Owen Wilson has struggled with his love life and mental health, there has also been a lot of controversy surrounding his family life. Wilson doesn't know his daughter Lyla and Varunie Vongsvirates said that Wilson hasn't even met her once.

Although no one knows the real story here unless Wilson confirms or denies his ex-girlfriend's claims, this is definitely proof that having a lot of money in the bank doesn't stop someone from having a troubled life.

Inside Owen Wilson's Relationships

Owen Wilson's high net worth doesn't change the fact that he has had some difficult romantic relationships. Owen Wilson and Kate Hudson had a dramatic relationship in the mid-2000s, which appears to be one of his most significant romances.

Although their relationship was full of highs and lows, Kate Hudson talked about the actor in an interview with Vogue in 2007 and had nothing but kind words. She said:

“Owen is such a lovely person, and I love him dearly. And I wish him beautiful things — health and everything that he desires in life.”

When Was Owen Wilson's Suicide Attempt?

After Owen Wilson's heartbreaking suicide attempt in 2007, he shared that he suffered from depression from when he was 11 years old. He also shared that Andrew Wilson, his brother, was there for him after. Andrew came up with a manageable schedule for him afterward. It's heartbreaking to hear about these struggles and further proof that a high net worth doesn't stop anyone from experiencing them. According to Us Weekly, Wilson was also using cocaine at the time.

Wilson talked about how he was feeling with Esquire:

“I’ve been in sort of a lucky place of feeling pretty appreciative of things. I know everything’s kind of up and down, but when you get on one of these waves, you’ve gotta ride it as long as you can. … Feeling pretty grateful. Well, grateful’s one of those words that get used all the time. Appreciative. Of, you know, stuff.”
