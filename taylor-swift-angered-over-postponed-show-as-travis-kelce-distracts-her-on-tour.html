Taylor Swift Angered Over Postponed Show As Travis Kelce Distracts Her On Tour

 Posted on September 25, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  665 words  |  Martina Birk

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift had to cancel a concert in Argentina due to unsafe weather conditions, which disappointed her but she prioritizes safety.
  • Her boyfriend Travis Kelce revealed that they had dinner together on the night of the postponed concert, making the most of the situation.
  • Despite controversies surrounding Travis, Taylor and Travis remain serious about their relationship and have been open about it.

Taylor Swift has proven that she’ll perform rain or shine, but the singer recently had to cancel a performance in Argentina due to unsafe weather conditions. Her boyfriend Travis Kelce, who has joined her for some Eras tour stops, recently revealed Taylor’s disappointed reaction.

During the Wednesday, November 15 episode of his New Heights podcast, which he co-hosts with his older brother Jason Kelce, Travis revealed, “I got down there hoping I was going to see Taylor’s second show, but it got rained out – really it got thunder and lightning’d out.”

“She wasn’t too happy about it – she kind of prides herself on performing through weather and rain and things like that,” the NFL star continued. “But when it’s unsafe to her, her crew and everybody in the stadium, you have to do what you have to do.”

Travis Says Taylor’s Cancelled Concert Had A Silver Lining

Taylor originally announced via X (formerly known as Twitter) on Friday, November 10 that her second Argentina concert had to be postponed due to weather issues, as the rain made the electrical requirements dangerous. She also informed fans that a new date had already been scheduled.

“I love a rain show but I’m never going to endanger my fans or my fellow performers and crew,” Taylor wrote in her statement. “We’ve rescheduled tonight’s Buenos Aires show for Sunday due to the weather being so truly chaotic it would be unsafe to try and put on this concert. Good news is I get to stay in Argentina longer.”

The night of the original concert date, Taylor was photographed enjoying dinner with Travis. The athlete has been present for some of Taylor’s South America stop recently after she became a fixture at his Chiefs games.

During the New Heights episode, Travis admitted it can be difficult to find one-on-one time with Taylor, so he was grateful that he got that evening to themselves, even though it was the result of a postponed Eras concert.

“The first night I was there was really the only chance I had to go for dinner,” he said. “It was the night the show got postponed so we didn’t want to just go and have a blast throughout the city – like we didn’t care about the show – so we made sure we stayed in the hotel and kept to ourselves.”

“We got some good food, had some empanadas and steak. … They have good steak down there,” Travis added.

Taylor And Travis Are Getting Serious Despite His Reputation

Taylor and Travis have been very public with their romance since they began dating earlier this year. The athlete hasn’t been afraid to discuss their relationship as well as his future hopes on his podcast, but not everything he’s said has been taken positively.

Travis was recently the subject of backlash after his “breeder” comments resurfaced. Speaking to his brother on the podcast, Travis said he wanted to find a “breeder” to have kid with to make their mother happy. Travis wasn’t dating Taylor at the time.

The NFL star has also been criticized by his ex-girlfriend for allegedly cheating. Maya Benberry recently issued a public warning to Taylor Swift about Travis’ bad behavior, though the pop star has yet to directly comment on her boyfriend’s controversy.
