Britney Spears seemed to be as shocked as fans were when Sam Asghari filed for divorce earlier this month following only 14 months of marriage. But the pop star appears to be doing her best to be moving on from the relationship – including some serious partying – though it’s leaving Sam unphased.

Britney’s Divorce Party Has Reinforced Sam’s Decision To Separate

Last weekend, Britney posted a variety of content form her social media showcasing a “divorce party” she hosted to cheer herself up amid the split. But the content was notably provocative. In one clip, Britney was seen rolling around on a bed topless amongst the sheets. At one point, she kicked her leg up into the air to reveal pink underwear and thigh-high boots.

Another video showed the former Disney star having her leg licked by a mystery man. A voice behind the camera could be heard saying, “Start at the boot bro.”

More footage showed Britney in a sheer green mini dress being carried by aa group of shirtless men near her outdoor pool.

“When you go to meet up with a so called ‘friend’ and drive an hour for chicken !!! Then you have to wait in the car and need to use the bathroom,” she captioned the post, which was made on Sunday. “I knew paps were tipped off because the car I was in was never used before … so what does a b*tch like me do !!??! I put my green dress on and show up at my friends !!! I invited my fav boys over and PLAYED ALL NIGHT !!!” she continued.

Although Sam hasn’t directly commented on Britney’s so-called “divorce party,” the Lioness star only feels confident in his decision to divorce following Brit’s latest online behavior.

“Sam isn’t sure if Britney was trying to get his attention on social media by throwing a divorce party and partying with other men, but it’s none of his business,” a source told Us Weekly. “It only reinforces his feelings on the split and knowing he did the right thing.”

Sam Denies Wanting Money In His Divorce From Britney

Sam filed for divorce earlier this month citing “irreconcilable differences,” though reports suggested Sam believes Britney was cheating on him with an employee, though he denies it.

However, he’s been accused of using the divorce as a way to get a big payout from Britney, despite there being a prenup in place. While the prenup reportedly entitled him to $1 million only after two years of marriage, which they haven’t reached, it’s been reported that Sam wants to challenge the agreement. One report claims he’s trying to negotiate with Britney in exchange for not releasing personal information about her to the public.

“[Sam is] attempting to negotiate concessions beyond his prenup and threatening to go public with extraordinarily embarrassing information about Britney unless he gets paid,” an insider claimed to Page Six. “So that’s what Sam is focused on,” they added.

Sam has denied allegations he’s challenging the prenup he shares with Britney. "There are many claims that Sam is challenging the prenup and threatening to exploit his ex-wife with videos," Sam’s representative said in a statement. "However, all these claims are false, as no negative intention has ever been directed towards her and never will be,” he continued, adding, "Sam has always and will always support her."

Sam and Britney’s divorce remains ongoing.
