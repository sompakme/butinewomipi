Logan Henderson is an American actor, singer, and songwriter.

Logan Henderson's breakthrough role came on the television series Big Time Rush, on which he portrayed the role of Logan Mitchell. He and the rest of the cast of Big Time Rush made an appearance at the 2010 and 2013 Kids' Choice Awards and performed at the 2011 Kids' Choice Awards.

He signed with Sony Music Entertainment and Columbia Records in 2009. Big Time Rush released singles such as "Big Time Rush", "City Is Ours", "Halfway There", and "Til I Forget About You".

The group released their debut album in 2010. The album charted at number 3 on the Billboard 200, and at #1 on the iTunes digital albums list. It was later certified Gold in the U.S. and Mexico.

In 2011, they released their second album Elevate. He co-wrote the song, "Time of Our Life" with Nicholas "Ras" Furlong, "Music Sounds Better With U", "Love Me Love Me" and "Superstar".

Logan Phillip Henderson was born on September 14, 1989 in North Richland Hills, Texas, USA.
