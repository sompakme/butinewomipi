If High Heels Are Too Painful For You These Footwear Options Are Just As Stunning

 Posted on August 22, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  907 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The only way to get around the discomfort and pain of wearing high heels is to simply not wear them. Thankfully, women have found alternatives to stilettos and pumps by wearing way more comfortable footwear options that make an outfit just as fashionable.

While our obsession with shoes may reach the level of Sex and the City's Carrie Bradshaw, it's simply unrealistic to wear Manolo Blahniks or Jimmy Choos every day. And, for those women who just can't stand wearing heels, numerous styles complement that LBD just like a five-inch heel would.

If high heels are just too painful for you, relax: there are options! And with so many options of styles and colors, you won't be breaking the bank to find the perfect pair of shoes that will complement any outfit.

Check out these 10 awesome footwear options for women who aren't fans of wearing high heels, but still want to look cute.

10 Go Fancy With Flats

You don't have to have a shoe warehouse like Celine Dion has for her massive collection of shoes, but when it comes to flats, there are so many different options that you can't go wrong with buying a few pairs that will match just about anything. Wearing high heels for an entire night can be quite painful, so many women take a pair of flat shoes along with them, especially if they are going to a wedding.

9 High-Quality Espadrilles Are Perfect For Spring And Summer

Espadrilles are a great spring and summer shoe that looks great with a pair of light-wash jeans, skirts, and dresses. They are highly comfortable shoes and come in a variety of colors and styles. The sole of an espadrille show can be platform, wedge, or flat depending on the style you are looking for.

8 Lace-Up Sandals Never Go Out Of Style

Lace-up sandals or gladiator sandals are trendy summer shoes that are a great alternative to high heels. Choose a neutral pair like black or beige, which go with pretty much anything, from high waisted shorts to loose dresses like the one above. They are chic flat shoes so they are very easy to walk in.

7 Flat Mules Look More Polished

Wearing a mule is a great option for the office because they look sophisticated and refined. A mule shoe has no back or constraint around the foot's heel, so they are a fantastic alternative to a heel during warmer weather. These shoes can be worn for any occasion and won't be going out of style anytime soon.

6 Slide Sandals Are Stylish

Slide sandals have become a very popular alternative to wearing high heels, especially when going on vacation. Why would anyone want to wear uncomfortable and painful high heels while at a tropical destination that's meant to be a relaxing trip? Slide sandals are the perfect substitute to heels since they look good with flowy dresses you'll wear on a night out and while you're enjoying daytime activities.

5 Sneakers And Dresses Are Now A Trend

Wearing sneakers with dresses is a trend that'll last forever. Pair a casual summer dress with your favorite pair of Converses or Nike Air Force 1s to make your look a lot edgier. It is a trend that celebrities love and it isn't tricky to pull off. Find a sweater dress, an LBD, or a cute chambray mini dress and pair it with a pair of cute white sneakers!

4 Kitten Heels Are More Realistic For Walking

If you want to wear heels but are worried about the pain that will come with them, opt to wear a shoe with a kitten heel. These heels are no longer associated with older women and have gained popularity because they are more realistic to wear if you have to do a lot of walking around. The kitten heel has transformed over the years and we've seen booties, mules, and sandals with this smaller heel.

3 Block Heels Are Sensible

Block heels are all the rage among women because of how comfortable they are. Block heels are a wider heeled shoe making them easier to walk around in and are a lot less painful than a typical high heel. These shoes offer more support and stability and are a terrific alternative to stilettos. Wear a pair of strappy open-toed block heels to a wedding or event and we bet you'll have them on all night.

2 A Pointer Flat Gives The Illusion Of A Date Night Shoe

A pointer flat will look a lot more elegant than a typical flat shoe, making them perfect for a dinner date. Not only are they way more comfortable than a high heel, but they are also just as chic and stylish to pair with a tighter fitting dress or a pair of dark jeans.

1 Wedges Are A Great Option If You Don't Want Your Feet To Suffer

If you can't decide between wearing high heels or flats, opt to wear a wedged shoe. These shoes are perfect for the summer and go great with white jeans, flirty dresses, and shorts. Wedges are more stable than heels and the thick wedge makes you feel like you are walking on level ground.
