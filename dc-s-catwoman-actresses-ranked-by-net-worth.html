DC's Catwoman Actresses, Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on July 30, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  921 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Batman is a hero that has been around seemingly since the dawn of time. The character first debuted in the DC graphic novels back in 1939, followed by both cinematic and television appearances in 1966. This beloved hero has had his story told and retold in numerous ways, and in many of them, we are introduced to his feline-esque friend: Catwoman.

Just as there have been many interpretations of Batman, there are several retellings of Catwoman. Starting in the '60s all the way through this year, there have been numerous actresses who have embodied Catwoman’s character. From the early actress Lee Meriwether to our current Zoë Kravitz, here are the last six decades of Catwomen ranked by their net worths.

9 The 'Gotham' Series Star Camren Bicondova Is Worth $1.5 Million

In 2014, Fox premiered its dark and dramatic take on the Batman story with the television series Gotham. Camren Bicondova was hired to portray Catwoman in all five seasons of the show. Prior to this role, she only had three acting credits, and has added two more since the first season aired. Camren was in a spin-off short called Gotham Stories in 2016 where she reprised her role as Selina/Catwoman, bringing her net worth to $1.5 million.

8 Lee Meriwether, The First Cinematic Catwoman, Has A $2 Million Net Worth

Lee Meriwether has lived an accomplished 86 years of life. Meriwether began her acting career in 1954, racking up an impressive 123 titles on her resume. The first Batman film came out in 1966, in which she portrayed The Catwoman, and she has since been constantly brought into the franchise. Lee has cameoed in Batman (the TV series) in 1967, Batman Vs. Two-Face in 2017, and Rise of the Catwoman in 2018. Her net worth rests at $2 milion.

7 Eartha Kitt's Net Worth Was $4 Million When She Passed

Towards the end of the Batman TV series, in the last season, Eartha Kitt stepped in to take over the Catwoman role for Julie Newmar. Kitt unfortunately passed away in 2008, leaving behind a net worth of $4 million. Besides acting, in which she did from 1948 until the time she passed, she was also a well-known singer and widely loved for her portrayal of Yzma in Disney’sThe Emperor’s New Groove.

6 Adrienne Barbeau Of The Animated Series Is Worth $5 Million

Adrienne Barbeau is a talented author, actress, singer, and Broadway performer. Her Catwoman portrayal was on Batman: The Animated Series, from 1992-1995. She has also been in the Broadway show “Grease” as Rizzo and been hired for over 150 movies and TV shows. Barbeau is currently still acting, with a movie called The Pitch-Fork in pre-production. All of these ventures have raised her net worth to $5 million.

5 The Newest Catwoman, Zoë Kravitz, Has A Net Worth Of $10 Million

She may come from a famous father, but Zoë Kravitz has paved her own way in Hollywood. Kravitz broke through in franchise work in X-Men: First Class in 2011, although she had been hired on almost a dozen titles before then. Perhaps a little-known fact, Zoë actually reprised her role as Catwoman in this year’s The Batman since she was first cast as the character in the animated The Lego Batman Movie. Her net worth has risen to a respectable $10 million.

4 'The Dark Night Rises' Star Anne Hathaway Has An $80 Million Net Worth

Anne Hathaway was Catwoman in The Dark Knight Rises in 2012, but she has many other impressive titles on her resume. Les Misérables, Oceans 8, Alice in Wonderland, and of course The Princess Diaries movies, are some of her best-known work. Hathaway has been involved in many franchises, helping to lift her net worth to $80 million. Always keeping busy, Anne also has three titles in post-production, three in pre-production, and one movie just announced.

3 The Controversial Catwoman Halle Berry's Net Worth Is $90 Million

Yet another Catwoman who was part of the X-Men franchise, Halle Berry starred as the titular character in the 2004 flop Catwoman. Berry was well-versed in Hollywood prior to this movie, and has been in many projects since, including portraying “Storm” in X-Men: The Last Stand and X-Men: Days of Future Past. She is also involved in the John Wick franchise and starred in the recent Hollywood hit Moonfall, bringing her net worth to $90 million.

2 1966's Catwoman Julie Newmar Is Worth $100 Million

Julie Newmar was among the original Catwomen, portraying The Catwoman in the Batman TV series between 1966-1967. As a woman in her late 80s, Newmar has an impressive list of titles on her resume, ranging from Batman projects to Seven Brides for Seven Brothers to an episode of the first Star Trek series. Her 78 acting credits have attributed to her $100 million net worth.

1 'Batman Returns' Star Michelle Pfeiffer Has A $250 Million Net Worth

The wealthiest Catwoman is Michelle Pfeiffer of the 1992 film Batman Returns. Pfeiffer has dipped her toe into the waters of several big-name franchises. Not only is she a big name for DC, but she has also worked for Marvel, acted in Grease 2, and took part in Hairspray. Michelle currently has a comfortable net worth of $250 million.
