Celebrities Who Stayed Friends After Dating The Same Person

 Posted on July 14, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  824 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Though some celebrities, like Ed Sheeran, Joe Jonas, and Meryl Streep, have found love with people who are not famous, others have gravitated towards fellow stars who understand their lifestyle. Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds; Emily Blunt and John Krasinski; Catherine Zeta-Jones and Michael Douglas; Angela Bassett and Courtney B. Vance; and Beyoncé and Jay-Z are all celebrity power couples who have been married for at least a decade - which is impressive by Hollywood's standards.

Related: Miley Cyrus Has Dated Some Big Names, But Who Is Her Richest Ex?

Still, Hollywood is small, and the chances of dating a famous friend's famous ex can be high. Sometimes, dating the same person can cause conflicts among stars. Fan comparisons and cheating allegations can make it difficult for celebrities to get along with their ex's new partner. Continue reading to find out which stars have managed to stay friends despite having dated the same person.

8 Hailey Bieber And Kendall Jenner

Hailey Bieber and Kendall Jenner have been besties for years now, and not even the fact that they both dated Justin Bieber could tear them apart. Allegedly, before Justin and Hailey got married in 2018, Kendall briefly dated Justin in 2015. Not only have Kendall and Hailey stayed extremely close, but Kendall and Justin also seem to still get along well as friends. All three of them even filmed an Instagram Live together during the pandemic lock-downs.

7 Drake And Justin Bieber

Hailey Bieber seems to have a thing for Canadians. Before she married Canadian superstar Justin Bieber, Hailey was linked to Drake. After Justin and Hailey broke up in 2016, the model was spotted hanging out with Drake. While fans believe that Justin expressed his jealousy with the alleged relationship in a remix of Drake's "Hotline Bling," Drake and Justin seem to be on good terms now. Justin and Hailey even starred in Drake's video for "POPSTAR" in 2020.

6 Selena Gomez And Miley Cyrus

Miley Cyrus reportedly dated Nick Jonas from 2006 to 2008. Selena Gomez then dated Nick from 2009 to 2010. Given their shared dating history, it would be understandable if Selena and Miley did not get along. However, earlier this year, Miley and Selena proved that they are still friends. Selena did an impression of "one of [her] oldest friends," Miley, during her SNL opening monologue. Miley then posted about the sweet shout out on her Instagram.

5 Gigi Hadid And Taylor Swift

Back in 2008, Taylor Swift and Joe Jonas dated. Joe supposedly broke up with Taylor over the phone, and Taylor has written a few songs about their relationship. Fortunately, Taylor and Joe have since put their past behind them. Taylor's bestie Gigi Hadid also dated Joe in 2015. Taylor and then-boyfriend Calvin Harris even went on a double date with Joe and Gigi. Gigi and Joe broke up after a few months, while Gigi and Taylor have remained close friends.

4 Selena Gomez And Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift and Selena Gomez do not let boys get in the way of their friendships. Selena is also one of Taylor's closest friends. Like with Gigi, Taylor and Selena share an ex. Selena Gomez dated Twilight's Taylor Lautner in 2009. After they broke up, the Taylors got together. While neither of them ended up with Taylor Lautner, Selena and Taylor have remained best friends.

3 Nikki Reed And Nina Dobrev

Former Vampire Diaries costars Nina Dobrev and Ian Somerhalder dated for over three years before breaking up in 2013. Some fans found it suspicious when Ian then married Nina's friend Nikki Reed in 2015. However, in 2017, Nikki, Nina, and Ian squashed the rumors about there being any bad blood among them. Nina posted a picture with Nikki and Ian with the caption "Farewell dinner with team Somereed! So good catching up with these goofballs."

2 Courteney Cox And Jennifer Aniston

On-screen Friends besties Courteney Cox and Jennifer Aniston are also best friends in real life, and sharing an ex has not stopped them from being there for each other. Jennifer and Counting Crows musician Adam Duritz reportedly dated in 1995. Courteney then dated Adam a couple of years after his relationship ended with her costar. Courtney and Adam allegedly met when she starred in the Counting Crows' music video for "A Long December."

1 Drew Barrymore And Kristen Wiig

Drew Barrymore was totally supportive when her friend Kristen Wiig began dating her ex-boyfriend, The Strokes' Fabrizio Moretti. Drew and Fabrizio dated for five years before breaking up in 2007. Then, in 2011, Kristen and Fabrizio's relationship began. Before Kristen and Fabrizio's 2013 break-up, Drew expressed her support for their relationship to Allure. She said, "It seems fitting that they would find each other. I was like, 'Right, of course, that makes perfect sense.'"
