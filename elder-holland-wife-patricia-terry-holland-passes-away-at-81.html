Her remarkable legacy will undoubtedly continue to resonate and inspire for generations to come.

In a heartfelt statement, church officials expressed their deep admiration for Sister Holland, portraying her as a woman of faith and unwavering dedication to her beliefs. 

They emphasized her profound commitment to serving both her faith and her community, as well as her boundless love and devotion to her family, a legacy that will be cherished forever.

At this moment, the specific cause of Sister Holland’s passing has not been made public. 

As her family copes with the loss, details regarding the arrangements for her funeral remain pending, allowing friends and well-wishers to offer their condolences and support during this difficult time.

Utah Governor Spencer Cox referred to Sister Holland as one of the kindest and most caring people on this earth.
