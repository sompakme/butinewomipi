Lo Bosworth Dating Jeremy Globerson: &quot;He's Very Nice&quot;

 Posted on July 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  145 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

It's love for Lo Bosworth! The former star of MTV's Laguna Beach and The Hills is dating businessman Jeremy Globerson, the California native tells Us Weekly. The two have been dating since 2012 and attended the 6th Annual Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic in Jersey City, New Jersey, June 1.

Though Bosworth and Globerson didn't pose for red carpet photos, the former reality star did post a picture of her beau via Instagram. (Bosworth has also posted pictures of herself with Globerson watching The Bachelorette and attending a friend's wedding.) According to a source, Bosworth and Globerson share an apartment in New York City.
