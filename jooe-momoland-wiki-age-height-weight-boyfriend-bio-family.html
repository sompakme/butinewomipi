Momoland Group, Singing Career, Songs List & Net worth

JooE starts her career in the field of singing when she became a member of Momoland group. The Momoland group was created by MLD Entertainment Company in 2016.

When the group created there are seven members are included named Yeonwoo, Jane, Nayun, JooE, Ahin, Hyebin, and Nancy.

The group first appeared in the reality T.V show named “Finding Momoland” in 2016. This show was mostly loved by people and the group also became the winner of this show.

After that on March 28, 2018, two new members were added to the group named Daisy and Taeha. She also performed in the Korean TV show named “King of Masked Singer”. In this show, she sings a song wearing a helicopter mask. JooE is a permanent member of Momoland group and works in every music video of the group.

She appeared in the song “Wonderful Love” which was released on 26 January 2017. After that, she makes so many singles, Reality shows, Music videos, and Stage shows with the group. She always performed in the group but only there is one music video of Kim Youngchul where she works with Hyebin and Yeonwoo.

The famous Korean show Kim Saeng Min’s Veteran appointed JooE as the cast member. She is also the brand ambassador of a popular makeup brand (Baker 7). She appeared in “Get It Beauty 2018” show as the master of ceremony. Lee Joo Won also emerge as a guest in Salty Tour on 28 April 2018.
